Privacy Policy for Date Shield

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Date Shield is a browser extension developed by Arthiva Labs to help users identify potential warning patterns when reviewing online dating profiles and selected conversation or message text.

This Privacy Policy explains how Date Shield handles information when you use the extension.

1. Information You Choose to Analyze

Date Shield may process text that you choose to submit or analyze through the extension, such as dating profile text or selected conversation and message text.

This information is used to provide the analysis requested by you and to identify potential warning patterns that may be relevant to online dating, romance scams, or deceptive behavior.

2. Purpose of Analysis

Date Shield is designed to identify and explain potential warning patterns, which may include financial requests, gift card requests, cryptocurrency or investment pitches, emergency narratives, rapid emotional escalation, repeated avoidance of meetings or video calls, and similar patterns.

Date Shield provides informational guidance only. Its analysis does not prove that a person is fraudulent, deceptive, unsafe, or trustworthy. Users should use their own judgment when making personal or relationship decisions.

3. Personal Information

Users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive personal information when using Date Shield. Only provide the text that is reasonably necessary for the analysis you want to perform.

4. Browser Permissions

Date Shield may request browser permissions that are necessary for its extension features to function. These permissions are used only to provide the functionality described by the extension.

5. Data Sharing and Selling

Arthiva Labs does not sell personal information submitted through Date Shield for advertising purposes.

Information may be processed by service providers when technically necessary to operate specific Date Shield features. Such processing is limited to providing the relevant functionality.

6. Payments

If you purchase access to Date Shield, payment transactions may be handled by third-party payment providers. Arthiva Labs does not directly process or store your complete payment card or banking credentials.

Payment providers process payment information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

7. Data Security

Arthiva Labs takes reasonable measures to protect information handled in connection with Date Shield. However, no electronic system or method of transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Date Shield is not intended for children under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to Date Shield, its features, or applicable requirements. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Date Shield's privacy practices, please contact Arthiva Labs through the support contact information provided on the official Arthiva Labs website or Date Shield listing.
