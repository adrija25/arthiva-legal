# Habit Streak Tracker Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 19, 2026**

Habit Streak Tracker is developed and provided by Arthiva Labs.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Habit Streak Tracker mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

Habit Streak Tracker is designed to minimize the collection of personal information.

The app allows users to create habits, record daily habit completions, track streaks, and view progress history.

Habit and progress information created within the app is stored locally on the user's device.

Arthiva Labs does not directly collect, transmit, or store this habit-tracking information on its own servers.

## 2. Account Information

Habit Streak Tracker does not currently require users to create an account or provide personal information such as their name, email address, phone number, or address.

## 3. Payments and Purchases

Habit Streak Tracker may offer optional premium features through a one-time Lifetime Pro in-app purchase.

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. Arthiva Labs does not directly collect or store users' payment card or banking information.

The app may receive information from Google Play indicating whether a valid purchase has been completed or previously purchased in order to unlock or restore premium features.

## 4. Notifications

Users may optionally enable daily habit reminders.

If enabled, the app uses the device's notification functionality to deliver reminders. Users can disable reminders within the app or manage notification permissions through their device settings.

## 5. Data Storage

Habit names, habit completion records, streak information, preferences, and related app data are stored locally on the user's device.

Deleting the app may result in the loss of locally stored app data unless the device or operating system provides an applicable backup mechanism.

## 6. Data Sharing

Arthiva Labs does not sell users' personal information.

Habit Streak Tracker does not directly share users' locally stored habit-tracking data with advertisers or data brokers.

Information necessary to process in-app purchases may be handled by Google Play as part of its payment and billing services.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Habit Streak Tracker uses Google Play services for distribution of the app and processing of in-app purchases.

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Habit Streak Tracker is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

Arthiva Labs does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through the app.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable measures to design the app in a way that minimizes unnecessary collection and transmission of user information.

However, no method of electronic storage or digital technology can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app, its features, or applicable requirements.

Any updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
